Story 3

A fox catches a rabit in it's mouth the rabbit screams "please I am pregnant!!! just let me give birth to this litter in a few days and I will let you eat me then" the fox replies "ok direct me to your den and I will leave you there and then come back in a few days after your children can survive on their own" "THANK YOU" screams the Rabbit the fox drops the rabbit off. A week or two later the fox returns to the Rabbits den. Once again the Rabbit thanks the fox emensely "thank you for having a little kindness in this terrible game of life" the fox replies "this world is cold enough I just wanted to make it a little warmer, can I see the life that can exist as a result of my kindness?" "Of course!!!"" exclaimed the Rabbit. The Rabbit brings the fox to the children he has allowed to live on good faith also showing him extra food she has collected for him. The fox tells the Rabbit her children are beutiful creations. And thanks her for the extra food as it was not a part of the deal. Then the fox gives the Rabbit an odd stare and she knows it's time. But then, the fox devours the children 1 by one has the Rabbit screams in desperation and agony. After he finishes the children he looks at the Rabbit and says "a deals a deal, I brought you back to your den and waited two weeks now you must let me eat you" the Rabbit lies there defeated and lacking any drive to live. The fox proceeds to eat the Rabbit, but as the fox leaves the cave he stops and drops dead. Turns out niether side had any intentions of holding up their end of the deal; the Rabbit had injested poisonus berries the moment she saw the fox arrive.
